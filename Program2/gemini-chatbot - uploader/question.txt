First Aid Questions (Nursing Certificate Level - Semester 1)

1.  Define first aid and explain its primary objectives in an emergency situation.

2.  List and briefly describe the "DRSABCD" action plan in first aid.

3.  What are the key differences between a sprain and a strain? How would you provide initial first aid for each?

4.  Describe the steps you would take to manage an adult who is choking but still conscious.

5.  Explain the R.I.C.E. principle and when it should be applied.

6.  Outline the signs and symptoms of anaphylaxis. What immediate first aid intervention is crucial?

7.  How would you differentiate between a first, second, and third-degree burn? What is the initial first aid for a small second-degree burn?

8.  A patient presents with a deep laceration on their arm. What are your immediate first aid priorities?

9.  Describe the correct procedure for performing CPR on an adult.

10. What are the common signs and symptoms of a stroke? Why is time critical in managing a stroke victim?

11. Explain how to correctly apply a pressure bandage for a bleeding wound.

12. What is shock, and what are the general signs and symptoms? How would you position a patient in shock?

13. List at least five items that should be included in a basic first aid kit for a home or small office.

14. Describe the first aid management for a person experiencing a seizure.

15. What steps would you take if you suspected a patient has suffered a head injury?

16. How would you provide first aid for a patient with a suspected fractured limb?

17. Explain the importance of maintaining body temperature in a hypothermic patient. What first aid measures would you implement?

18. What are the signs of a severe allergic reaction, and what is the primary first aid medication used in such cases?

19. Describe the first aid for a snake bite in your region (assume a general principle if unsure of specific region).

20. Why is it important to document first aid interventions, even in non-medical settings?
